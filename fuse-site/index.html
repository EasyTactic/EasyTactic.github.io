Welcome to the new health economy
FUSE is the first and only quantitative artificial intelligence healthcare platform to leverage information and incentives in a secure ecosystem with a unique blockchain currency that will optimize health outcomes.

why fuse?

FUSE is the first quantitative healthcare AI platform that will:

  • Assist patients in finding the right healthcare provider in a timely manner
  • Provide a monetized blockchain-based currency (FUSE tokens) to incentivize patients for making smart health decisions
  • Enable providers and payors to provide more efficient care
  • Reduce the overall cost of care for patients and payors
  • Enable patients to control their medical data via Decentralized Personal Patient Medical Records (built on HIPAA & GDPR compliant permissioned blockchain)

FUSE for Patients

  • Patients will earn real interchangeable FUSE tokens for healthy behaviors like scheduling and keeping appointments
  • Provide concierge scheduling and eligibility confirmation
  • Allow access to the largest database of healthcare providers in the United States with a significant number of performance analytics and reviews

FUSE for Healthcare Providers

FUSE will be free to providers and will promote access to:

  • New patients who have been screened for eligibility with all demographics prechecked
  • Patients who are incentivized with FUSE currency to come on time and not cancel appointments
  • Education and control of reputation management tools

THE US HEALTHCARE SYSTEM IS BROKEN.
IT’S TIME TO FIX IT!

The United States ranked highest on the list of the 20 Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development (OECD)1 countries for health care expenditure, but last for coverage.

FUSE is called upon to build an ecosystem that will merge insulated healthcare pieces in one settled mechanism by providing smooth data flow between agents and stakeholders.

Health care costs continue to rise in the U.S. and throughout the developed world. Total U.S. health care expenditures were estimated to be $3.24 trillion in 2015, and are projected to soar to $3.78 trillion in 2018.

A survey of older people in 11 countries finds that U.S. adults are sicker than their counterparts abroad, as well as the most likely to have problems paying their medical bills and getting needed healthcare.

U.S. adults also reported difficulty getting care in a timely fashion and using emergency departments for issues that a primary care physician could treat.

Approximately 200,000 Americans die from preventable medical errors including facility-acquired conditions.

A NEW HEALTHCARE ECOSYSTEM

FUSE is determined to build a better healthcare ecosystem that will merge insulated healthcare pieces in one settled mechanism by providing smooth data flow between all stakeholders.

FUSE Healthcare Ecosystem will:

  • Improve patient access by enabling patients to access the right provider in a convenient manner
  • Improve practice efficiency by eliminating no-shows, cancellations and long waiting times
  • Improve communications by creating a efficient methodology for bilateral communications between patients and their providers
  • Improve adherence by incentivizing patients to achieve more optimal behavior and outcomes
  • Improve transparency by providing decentralized personal patient medical records

FUSE DECENTRALIZES PERSONAL PATIENT MEDICAL RECORDS

  • Returns control of personal healthcare data to the hands of the patient
  • Reduces overspends by eliminating unnecessary and redundant services which, in turn, saves time to patients and money to insurance companies
  • Helps healthcare providers serve patients better

FUSE TOKENS (FUSE CURRENCY)

Token type: ERC20 /Phase I/

FUSE tokens will be supplied to patients as earned rewards for:

  • Making healthy lifestyle choices including diet and exercise
  • Adhering to follow-ups and arriving punctually to scheduled appointments
  • Participating in FUSE referral programs
  • Active usage of FUSE APP (eg, watching and learning provided content, participating in health surveys for CQI, reviewing health providers, uploading information about their health)

FUSE tokens will also be available for purchase to a variety of third party vendors who want access to the FUSE HEALTH ECONOMY, including:

  • Marketers of health-related products
  • Health insurance providers
  • Research companies
  • Pharmaceutical and biotech companies

FUSE tokens will be spent by:

Patients

  • VIP concierge service (eg, right match with the right doctor, additional screening, free rides to healthcare providers, free premium gym membership)
  • Healthy products and services (eg, fresh juices, healthy food, spa, IV-infusions)
  • Visits, co-pays, co-insurance, deductibles, and health insurance-related payments

Providers

  • E-post
  • Push notifications for patients in their area

FUSE CORPORATE MISSION

Our mission is to foster a healthier population that will help improve overall health outcomes while reducing costs to the benefit of all stakeholders in the US healthcare system.

Actionable measures include:

  • Promoting healthy lifestyle and educating people about disease prevention
  • Reducing healthcare-related costs to all Americans
  • Decreasing costs for payors and providers
  • Working with US government agencies to help fix the healthcare system
  • Becoming the shield (advocate) for patients
